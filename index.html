Committed to Water Quality & Safety

At AquaFlow, your health and safety are our top priority. We adhere strictly to the Ghana Standards Authority (GSA) DGS 175:2021 for drinking water quality. Our water is sourced from approved, safe locations and undergoes rigorous testing.

  • Sourced from approved, tested wells.
  • Regular quality checks by certified labs.
  • Tankers are meticulously cleaned and maintained.
  • Trained personnel ensure hygienic handling.

We provide water that is safe for drinking, cooking, and all household or commercial uses. Request a copy of our latest water quality report.
